Our Story

Medical inventions and stuff

The Steerable SATA Punch is the first fully detachable and therefore reusable arthroscopic cutter that has a sideways articulation range of 110 degrees. By rotating the shaft this innovative punch allows orthopedic surgeons to reach all areas inside the knee with only one instrument (instead of the currently used set of left and right pre-bended punches).

As the Steerable SATA Punch allows surgeons to use a low force approach when aligning the instrument tip with increased precision during cutting, high portal stresses that induces tissue trauma, pain and risk of infection can be prevented. The Steerable SATA Punch’s modular system design allows the surgeon to choose a preferred handle type, and the hospital to clean and maintain the instrument at minimum costs
